# 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

### 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to monitor and analyze key supply chain metrics, helping businesses improve operational efficiency, delivery performance, inventory management, and supplier effectiveness.


## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI solution built to provide real-time visibility into supply chain operations. It enables stakeholders to track revenue, orders, inventory levels, supplier performance, and delivery efficiency through interactive visualizations and KPI-driven insights.

This dashboard is intended for supply chain managers, business analysts, operations teams, and decision-makers seeking data-driven insights to optimize supply chain performance.


## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform for data visualization and dashboard development.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data extraction, transformation, and cleaning.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic analytics.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between datasets for accurate reporting and analysis.
* 📁 **File Formats** – `.pbix` for development and `.png/.jpg` for dashboard previews.


## 4. Data Source

**Source:** Sample Supply Chain Dataset

The dataset contains operational supply chain information including:

* Orders
* Revenue
* Product Categories
* Inventory Levels
* Supplier Information
* Delivery Performance
* Regional Distribution

The data was processed and transformed to create meaningful KPIs and business insights.


## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Supply chain operations involve multiple moving components including suppliers, inventory, logistics, and customer deliveries. Without a centralized monitoring system, organizations often struggle to identify operational inefficiencies, delayed shipments, inventory shortages, and underperforming suppliers.

Key questions such as:

* Which regions generate the highest revenue?
* Are deliveries being completed on time?
* Which suppliers are performing best?
* What products contribute most to revenue?
* Are inventory levels sufficient to meet demand?

can be difficult to answer using raw operational data.


### • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

* Monitor overall supply chain performance.
* Track revenue and order trends.
* Measure OTIF (On-Time In-Full) delivery performance.
* Analyze supplier effectiveness.
* Identify inventory and logistics bottlenecks.
* Support data-driven decision making.


### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### KPI Cards

Displays key business metrics including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* OTIF %
* Average Delivery Delay
* Inventory Levels

#### Revenue Analysis

Visualizes revenue distribution across regions and product categories, helping identify top-performing business segments.

#### Order Performance Tracking

Monitors order fulfillment and delivery efficiency through OTIF and delay analysis.

#### Inventory Monitoring

Tracks stock availability and inventory performance to identify potential shortages or excess stock.

#### Supplier Performance Dashboard

Evaluates suppliers based on operational KPIs and delivery effectiveness.

#### Regional Analysis

Provides a geographical view of revenue contribution and supply chain performance across different regions.


### • Business Impact & Insights

* Improved visibility into supply chain operations.
* Identified delivery delays affecting customer satisfaction.
* Highlighted top-performing suppliers and product categories.
* Enabled proactive inventory management.
* Supported strategic decisions through KPI-driven reporting.
